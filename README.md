# AgentStack — DeFAI Agent Hosting Platform

## What We Do
AgentStack is a hosted Decentralized AI (DeFAI) platform built natively on Arbitrum. We provide users with a dedicated, always-on AI trading agent (powered by Claude Sonnet and LangGraph) that continuously monitors, manages, and optimizes their DeFi portfolio. 

Users pay a monthly USDC subscription to keep their agent running and unlock specific protocol integrations ("skills") à la carte. Your funds never leave your custody—we leverage cutting-edge **EIP-7702 smart accounts** with scoped session keys. This allows our backend AI to execute specific, pre-approved strategies on your behalf, up to defined spend limits, without ever having the ability to withdraw your funds to external addresses.

## Our Progression: GMX Perps and Other Protocols
We are building a pluggable skill architecture, allowing our agents to progressively master the most lucrative protocols in DeFi:
- **Phase 1: Money Markets (Aave V4)** - Automated supply/borrow loops, yield routing, and autonomous health factor management (liquidation protection).
- **Phase 2: GMX V2 Perpetuals** - Advanced delta-neutral strategies, automated funding rate farming, and dynamic leverage management on GMX. Our agent will actively manage perp positions based on real-time market data.
- **Phase 3: Uniswap V4 & Pendle** - Automated concentrated liquidity (LP) position rebalancing, hook-driven trading, and yield token (PT/YT) arbitrage on Pendle.
- **Phase 4: Multi-Strategy Rebalancing** - Cross-protocol yield routing and complex multi-leg execution, dynamically moving capital to wherever the risk-adjusted yield is highest.

## AI Signals for Higher Subscriptions
We align our platform's capabilities with user needs through a tiered, on-chain USDC subscription model. Higher tiers unlock deeper AI insights and more complex execution logic:
- **Starter Tier:** Access to foundational skills (like Aave) for basic yield optimization and risk management.
- **Pro Tier:** Unlocks advanced protocol skills such as **GMX V2 Perpetuals** and Uniswap V4 LP management. Pro users receive enhanced AI market signals, predictive risk modeling, and tighter execution loops.
- **Elite Tier:** Full access to complex yield trading (Pendle) and multi-strategy rebalancing. Elite users benefit from high-frequency predictive AI signals, priority queue execution, and the most sophisticated autonomous portfolio defense mechanisms.

## Why We Are Needed in the Arbitrum Ecosystem
Arbitrum is the undisputed home of complex, high-yield DeFi (led by protocols like GMX, Camelot, and Pendle). However, maximizing yield and managing risk on these platforms requires constant monitoring, deep mathematical understanding, and split-second execution—creating a massive barrier to entry for everyday users.

AgentStack bridges this gap by bringing autonomous, secure AI agents to Arbitrum. We provide:
1. **Hands-Free Complexity:** Retail users can participate in sophisticated GMX perp strategies or Pendle yield trading without sitting at a screen 24/7.
2. **Automated Risk Mitigation:** Our agents poll the blockchain every 5 minutes. If a position's health factor drops or AI signals detect impending market volatility, the agent automatically unwinds or hedges the position.
3. **Efficient Ecosystem Liquidity:** By automating intelligent capital allocation, we drive sticky, highly efficient liquidity directly into Arbitrum's top protocols, strengthening the entire network.
